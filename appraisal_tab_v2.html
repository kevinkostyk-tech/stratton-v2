Note on AVMs and Listings:

Automated Valuation Models (AVMs) and active "For Sale" listings, while providing general market sentiment, are not considered reliable for formal appraisal purposes. Appraisers must use verifiable closed sales to support their conclusions. AVMs often lag market trends and do not account for property-specific features or conditions, while listing prices reflect seller aspirations, not confirmed market value.
